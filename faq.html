Frequently Asked Questions
What is covered by the registration costs?

Accomdations for the whole week in shared rooms, breakfast and lunch as well as an excursion on Wednesday. Students will only have to pay for the cost of travel to Bertinoro and for their dinners in the many cosy restaurant in the city.


When does the school start and end?

We start Saturday May 20th at 1:30pm and end on Saturday 27th at 12:00. Students are expected to stay the whole week.


Can I get support to attend the Summer School?

Yes! We will pay you to attend the summer school. We have funding from three sources: ACM's SIGPLAN, the National Science Foundation (NSF), and the Office of Naval Reseaerch (ONR). These sources come with their own restrictions: the NSF and ONR are limited to students residing in the USA. SIGPLAN aims to support diversity.

Do apply! Regardless of these limitations we will try to support you. Please be modest in your requests and try to save money wherever possible. If funded, check with the organizers before booking.


Who can attend?

The main target audience are students interested in programming languages. We particularly interested in encouraging minorities and also undergraduates. Anyone else is welcome space permitting.


How to apply?

Send us email, at the address listed on the registration page, with the information we require. Tell us why you are interested in the school and what your background is. If you need funding tell us how much you think you would need. Don't hesitate to ask questions.


Are there prerequisites?

An undergraduate programming language class and/or compiler class would is probably all you need.
